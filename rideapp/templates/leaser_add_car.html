Rideplex – Leaser Agreement

Rideplex – Leaser Agreement & Terms

By listing a vehicle on the Rideplex platform, you (“Leaser”) agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Vehicle Ownership and Authorization

The leaser confirms that the vehicle listed on the Rideplex platform is legally owned by them or that they have full legal authorization to rent the vehicle. The vehicle must comply with all applicable transport laws and regulations.

2. Required Documents

Before a vehicle can be listed or activated on Rideplex, the leaser must upload valid documents for verification, including:

  • Aadhar Card
  • Driving License
  • RC Book (Vehicle Registration Certificate)
  • Insurance Certificate
  • PUC Certificate
  • PAN Card

Rideplex reserves the right to verify, approve, or reject any submitted document.

3. Offline Verification Process

After adding a vehicle on the Rideplex platform, the leaser must visit the Rideplex offline verification location.

During this visit, the leaser must:

  • Bring the listed vehicle to the Rideplex location
  • Carry original documents and photocopies of all required personal and vehicle documents

Rideplex staff will verify the vehicle condition and documents before activating the vehicle for rental services.

4. Vehicle Condition Requirement

At the time of verification, the vehicle must be in good and roadworthy condition. Rideplex reserves the right to reject or delay activation if the vehicle does not meet safety or operational standards.

5. Earnings and Payment

The leaser earns income depending on how many times their vehicle is rented through the Rideplex platform.

Rideplex processes payments to the leaser monthly based on completed rides. A 15% platform fee is applied to each ride.

Rideplex does not guarantee a fixed number of bookings, as demand may vary depending on location, time, and user demand.

6. Vehicle Storage and Maintenance

After successful verification, the vehicle may remain parked at the Rideplex operational location when not in use.

  • The leaser (vehicle owner) is responsible for major maintenance such as servicing, mechanical repairs, and insurance renewal.
  • Rideplex will take reasonable care of the vehicle while it is parked at the Rideplex location.
  • If a vehicle remains unused for an extended period, the leaser may be contacted if maintenance or servicing is required before the vehicle can be rented again.

7. Vehicle Damage Responsibility

Self-Drive Rentals

If the vehicle is rented for self-drive and the renter causes damage, repair costs will be covered using the security deposit collected from the user , according to Rideplex policies.

In case of major accidents, applicable insurance coverage will be used.

Driver-Based Rentals

If the vehicle is rented with a Rideplex driver and damage occurs due to driver negligence, Rideplex will take responsibility for handling the situation.

Rideplex assigns experienced drivers, but in case of serious accidents, repair or claims will depend on the vehicle insurance policy uploaded by the leaser.

8. Vehicle Availability and Removal

  • The leaser cannot directly remove the vehicle from the platform dashboard.
  • If the leaser wishes to make the vehicle temporarily unavailable or remove it, they must contact the Rideplex admin team.

9. Vehicle Return Request

If a leaser wishes to have their vehicle returned , they must submit a request to the Rideplex admin team for vehicle return processing.

  • If the vehicle is currently on a ride, the request will be processed after the ride is completed.
  • Once the ride ends, the vehicle will be marked unavailable for bookings, and Rideplex return the vehicle to the leaser.

10. Vehicle Maintenance Responsibility

The leaser must ensure the vehicle remains in proper working condition. If a vehicle becomes unsafe or poorly maintained, Rideplex may temporarily disable the vehicle listing until the issue is resolved.

11. Misuse or Policy Violation

If a leaser submits false information, fake documents, or violates Rideplex policies, Rideplex reserves the right to suspend the account, remove the vehicle listing, or take necessary action without prior notice.

12. Platform Rights

Rideplex reserves the right to:

  • Verify all uploaded documents and vehicle details
  • Temporarily disable or remove vehicle listings when necessary
  • Enforce platform safety and operational policies

13. Agreement Confirmation

By clicking “I Agree”, the leaser confirms that they have read, understood, and accepted all the terms and conditions of the Rideplex platform.
